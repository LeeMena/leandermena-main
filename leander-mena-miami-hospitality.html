Professional Background

About Leander Mena

Leander Mena was raised in Miami and built his career entirely within South Florida's hospitality industry. He entered the field through restaurant operations and steadily advanced through progressively senior roles at independent concepts and internationally branded hotel properties, developing a comprehensive understanding of F&B management across every format — from high-volume standalone restaurants to full-service hotel outlets, banquet halls, and catering departments serving hundreds of guests at a time.

His career is defined by a specialty in complex, high-stakes situations: pre-opening builds where an entire department must be staffed, trained, and ready by a fixed date; turnaround engagements where existing programs are losing money or guest satisfaction; and growth situations where an operator needs experienced leadership without a permanent hire. This has made him a natural fit for fractional and consulting arrangements, where his institutional knowledge can be applied quickly and with measurable outcomes.

Leander Mena brings particular depth in labor management and cost control — two of the most persistent operational challenges in Miami's hospitality market, where seasonal swings, high turnover, and thin margins demand precision scheduling and real-time P&L awareness. He builds scheduling systems grounded in historical cover data and forward-looking event calendars, which consistently reduce overtime exposure without degrading service quality.

He has led pre-opening projects for both restaurant and hotel F&B programs, including the banquet and events launch at SLS Brickell Hotel & Residences, where he built the department's staffing structure, standard operating procedures, vendor relationships, and training programs from the ground up in the months before opening. That kind of structured, deadline-driven work — with clear deliverables and no margin for delay — is a signature of how Leander Mena approaches every engagement.

Currently based in Miami Shores, Florida, he works with independent restaurant operators, boutique hotel groups, and institutional F&B programs across Miami-Dade County as a fractional general manager, pre-opening consultant, and operations recovery specialist.
