Who Is Leander Mena

Miami Hospitality Leadership With a Proven Track Record

Leander Mena is a Miami-based hospitality operations leader whose career spans nearly two decades of hands-on work at South Florida's most demanding properties — from SLS Brickell and the InterContinental Miami to Viceroy, Maska, and Marabu. He has opened, stabilized, and restructured restaurants, hotel F&B outlets, banquet departments, and catering programs that serve thousands of covers per week.

The Miami hospitality market operates at an intensity few cities match: seasonal surges that double or triple cover counts overnight, an international guest base with exacting standards, and a competitive landscape where a single negative review cycle can damage revenue for quarters. Leander Mena has built his career mastering this environment — translating complex operational challenges into disciplined systems that keep teams performing and guests returning.

Today, Leander Mena offers fractional F&B operations leadership to Miami restaurants and hotel groups that need executive-level expertise without a full-time hire. Whether the assignment is a pre-opening build, a turnaround, or an interim GM engagement, he brings the same rigor and accountability that drove results at every property in his portfolio.

Miami F&B Operations: What Leander Mena Delivers

Every engagement is tailored to the property's specific stage, market position, and operational gap. These are the four pillars Leander Mena is most frequently retained to lead.

01

Pre-Opening Restaurant & Hotel F&B

From concept finalization through opening night, Leander Mena builds the operational infrastructure that new Miami restaurants and hotel F&B outlets need: staff recruitment and training, SOP development, POS configuration, vendor relationships, inventory par levels, and soft-opening management. Properties he opens are ready to run at full capacity from night one.

02

Fractional General Manager Services

Not every Miami property can justify a full-time GM salary. Leander Mena provides fractional executive leadership — available for a defined number of days per week or month — giving independent restaurants and boutique hotel F&B programs access to senior operational talent at a fraction of the full-time cost. Engagements typically run 3–12 months.

03

Operations Recovery & Turnaround

When Miami hospitality operations drift — guest scores declining, labor costs creeping above budget, staff turnover accelerating — Leander Mena conducts a structured operational audit, identifies the root causes, and implements a recovery plan. His turnaround work has restored profitability and team stability at properties that had been underperforming for multiple consecutive quarters.

04

Banquet & Catering Operations

Banquet and catering revenue is among the highest-margin F&B income available to Miami hotels and event venues. Leander Mena has directed banquet operations for properties executing hundreds of events per year — from intimate board dinners to 500-person wedding receptions — building the staffing models, execution playbooks, and client management systems that turn event revenue into consistent profit.
