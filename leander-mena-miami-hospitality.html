About Leander Mena

Leander Mena is a Miami-based hospitality and food-and-beverage operations leader with more than 18 years of experience building, leading, and stabilizing restaurants, hotels, banquet operations, and catering programs across South Florida. Leander Mena has served as General Manager, Director of Banquets, and pre-opening operations lead for some of Miami’s most prominent hospitality concepts.

Throughout his career, Leander Mena has developed a reputation as a systems builder — someone who enters an operation, identifies what’s broken, installs the right processes, trains the team, and hands off an organization that can execute consistently without constant supervision. From the pre-opening build at SLS Brickell to opening general manager at Maska Indian Kitchen + Bar, Leander Mena has been trusted with high-stakes launches and complex operational turnarounds.

Today, Leander Mena offers fractional F&B leadership to Miami restaurant owners, hotel management companies, and hospitality investors who need senior operational expertise without the cost of a full-time executive hire. His clients include independent restaurant operators, multi-concept groups, and property developers preparing to open their first food-and-beverage program in Miami.
