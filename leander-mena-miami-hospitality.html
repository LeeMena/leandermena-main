Professional Profile

Leander Mena

Hospitality & F&B Operations Leader

Miami, Florida  ·  18+ Years Experience

Leander Mena is a Miami-based hospitality and food-and-beverage operations leader with more than 18 years of hands-on experience managing restaurant, hotel, banquet, and catering operations across South Florida's most recognized properties.

His career spans Opening General Manager at Michelin-concept Maska Indian Kitchen + Bar, Director of Banquets (Pre-Opening) at SLS Brickell, General Manager at Marabu Restaurant, and F&B leadership roles at the Viceroy Hotel and InterContinental Miami.

Today, Leander offers fractional F&B leadership — giving independent restaurants, hotel groups, and multi-concept operators access to senior-level operational expertise without the overhead of a full-time executive hire.
