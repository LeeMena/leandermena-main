The Framework

The Miami Pre-Opening Playbook

Opening a restaurant or hotel F&B program is a high-stakes project with dozens of moving parts happening simultaneously. Most pre-opening failures come from underestimating how long hiring, SOP development, and training actually take — not from a lack of vision.

Leander Mena has led pre-opening operations at Maska Indian Kitchen + Bar, SLS Brickell, and multiple other Miami properties. He brings a structured, repeatable framework that eliminates the guesswork and builds a solid operating foundation from day one.

01

90–60 Days Out

Staffing model development, org chart finalization, job descriptions, hiring kick-off. Vendor sourcing and purchasing setup. Initial SOP framework outlined.

02

60–30 Days Out

Team hiring completion, SOP library built, training program designed and delivered. Menu walkthroughs with kitchen. Systems and technology configured.

03

30 Days → Opening

Dry runs, soft opening execution, real-time feedback loops. First 30 days of operations stabilization and handover to permanent leadership.
