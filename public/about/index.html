Where Vision Meets Operational Reality

Leander Mena is a fractional F&B Director and pre-opening consultant based in Miami, Florida. Over 18+ years he has opened, stabilized, and scaled restaurants, hotels, banquets, and catering operations across South Florida.

His track record includes properties like Maska, SLS Brickell, Marabu, Viceroy Miami, and InterContinental Miami - spanning Michelin-star concepts to large-scale luxury hotel F&B.
