Overview

A Career Built on the Floor

Leander Mena's hospitality career spans 18+ years of hands-on leadership across Miami's restaurant, hotel, and catering sectors. From pre-opening builds at luxury hotels to stabilizing high-volume independent restaurants, his track record is built on systems that work and teams that execute.

Every role listed below reflects real operational ownership — not advisory or consulting, but leading teams, managing P&L, and delivering measurable results on the floor.

2022 – Present
Opening General Manager
Maska Indian Kitchen + Bar — Miami, FL

Built the opening team from scratch for a high-profile Miami concept led by a Michelin-starred chef. Oversaw vendor onboarding, SOP development, front-of-house staffing, and training — taking the restaurant from pre-construction through a successful opening night.

2019 – 2022
Director of Banquets — Pre-Opening
SLS Brickell — Miami, FL

Joined the SLS Brickell pre-opening team as Director of Banquets, responsible for designing event staffing models, creating banquet execution systems and playbooks, and ensuring flawless delivery for large-scale hotel events before the property's first guest arrival.

2016 – 2019
General Manager
Marabu Restaurant | Coal Fire Cuban Cuisine — Miami, FL

Led full F&B operations for a high-volume Miami concept, with a focus on labor cost optimization, service consistency, and guest experience improvement. Reduced labor cost percentage while maintaining revenue growth through scheduling restructure and staff development.

2013 – 2016
F&B Operations Leader
Viceroy Hotel — Miami, FL

Oversaw food-and-beverage operations for a high-design Miami lifestyle hotel, managing restaurant, bar, and in-room dining programs. Led team training initiatives and implemented service standards aligned with the brand's luxury positioning.

2008 – 2013
Banquet & Catering Manager
InterContinental Miami — Miami, FL

Managed large-scale banquet and catering operations at one of Miami's flagship convention hotels. Coordinated multi-day events, corporate dinners, and private functions, overseeing service teams of 40+ staff and maintaining IHG brand standards throughout.
