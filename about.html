Background

Miami Hospitality, From the Ground Up

Leander Mena has spent 18+ years in the trenches of Miami's hospitality industry — opening restaurants from scratch, directing banquet operations at luxury hotels, and leading high-volume F&B programs across South Florida's most recognized properties.

His career spans roles as Opening General Manager, Director of Banquets, and F&B Director at properties including Maska Indian Kitchen + Bar, SLS Brickell, Marabu Restaurant, Viceroy Hotel, and InterContinental Miami.

Today, Leander offers his expertise on a fractional basis — giving independent restaurants, hotel groups, and multi-concept operators access to senior-level leadership without the cost of a full-time executive hire.
